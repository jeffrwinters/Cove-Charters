# User Personas

## Customer

The customer is booking a premium day on the water at Lake of the Ozarks. They may only book once per season, so the public web experience should be fast, clear, and SEO-friendly.

Primary needs:

- Browse premium boats
- Understand capacity, amenities, pricing, and fit
- Request a booking
- Choose an approved captain
- Sign required charter agreements
- Receive clear confirmation and trip details

## Captain

Captains operate trips for boats where they are approved. Legally, the customer must choose their own captain from the approved list.

Primary needs:

- Maintain availability
- Accept or decline booking requests
- Receive booking and agreement details
- Complete post-charter closeout
- View payout receipts/statements

## Boat Owner

Boat owners list boats in the Cove marketplace and receive payouts for completed charters.

Primary needs:

- Maintain boat profile and media
- Maintain approved captain list
- Track bookings
- Receive payout reporting
- Understand when boats are unavailable

## Back Office

Back office is a critical persona, not an afterthought. Back office coordinates the operational reality of the marketplace.

Primary needs:

- Change boats on bookings
- Change or assist with captain selection
- Facilitate customer communication
- Track agreements and attachments
- Review post-charter closeout
- Calculate and facilitate owner and captain payouts
- Override data when operationally necessary
