# Product Vision

Cove Charters is an upscale Lake of the Ozarks charter marketplace focused on premium boats, legally correct bareboat-style workflows, approved captain selection, and strong back-office operations.

## Product Positioning

Cove is not intended to be a generic pontoon rental marketplace. The product should feel curated, premium, and concierge-oriented.

## Core Concept

Customers browse premium boats, request a charter, and choose from captains approved for the selected boat. Cove coordinates the operational workflow between customers, boat owners, captains, and back office.

## Differentiators

- Upscale charter inventory
- Customer-selected captain workflow
- Boat-specific approved captain lists
- Charter agreement handling
- Back-office coordination
- Owner and captain payout support
- SEO-friendly public pages for boats and captains

## Product Principles

- Public website first, not customer mobile app first
- Customers may only book once or a few times per season
- Captains may eventually need operational mobile-friendly tools
- SEO should be treated as a first-class product requirement
- Legal workflow must respect bareboat charter requirements
- Back office must be able to override boats, captains, booking details, and payouts
