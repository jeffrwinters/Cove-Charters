# Guest Book (Light CRM)

## Vision

Cove should include a lightweight hospitality-focused customer relationship system called the **Guest Book** rather than a traditional CRM.

The goal is to help deliver exceptional repeat guest experiences, preserve institutional knowledge, and simplify repeat bookings.

## Design Principles

- Hospitality first
- Concierge experience
- Operational tool, not a sales tool
- Preserve guest preferences
- Make repeat bookings effortless

## Guest Profile

- Name
- Phone
- Email
- Address
- Emergency contact
- Referral source
- Preferred communication method
- Notes

## Charter History

- Every charter
- Lifetime spend
- Last charter date
- Boats chartered
- Captains selected
- Group size history

## Preferences

- Favorite boats
- Favorite captains
- Preferred marina
- Favorite restaurants/coves
- Watersports interests
- Music preferences
- Family vs party
- Special occasions
- Accessibility needs
- Pets

## Relationships

- Family members
- Friends that charter together
- Corporate groups
- Referral relationships

## Documents

- Signed agreements
- Waivers
- IDs (future)

## Future Concierge Features

- Birthday reminders
- Anniversary reminders
- 'Has not chartered in 18 months' reminders
- Memorial Day / holiday reminders
- Personalized concierge notes
- Marketing audiences based on preferences and history

## Product Goal

Guests should feel like Cove remembers them and their preferences every time they return, without feeling like they are in a traditional CRM. This should become one of Cove's primary competitive differentiators.
