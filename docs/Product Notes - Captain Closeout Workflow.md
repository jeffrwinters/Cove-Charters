# Cove Charters - Captain Closeout Workflow

These notes capture product decisions for the MVP so they are not lost.

## Captain Post-Charter Closeout
After every completed charter, the assigned captain completes a closeout form.

### Operational Fields
- Price per mile
- Miles travelled
- Fuel charges
- Gallons of fuel used
- Who paid for fuel? (Captain / Owner / Customer)
- Who paid for cleaning? (Captain / Owner / Customer)
- Additional notes

These values drive the financial reconciliation performed by the back office.

## Back Office
The back office reviews the submitted closeout before finalizing payouts.
Future enhancements include editing values, approving/rejecting submissions, and recording actual payouts.

## Captain Payout Statement
After reconciliation, the captain should have access to a payout receipt showing:

- Charter payout
- Fuel reimbursement or customer fuel charge
- Cleaning adjustments
- Additional reimbursement or deductions
- Total payout
- Gross revenue collected
- Gross profit
- Estimated tax
- Cove profit

The receipt should be printable, downloadable as PDF, and available from the captain portal.

## Workflow
Booking
→ Charter Complete
→ Captain Closeout
→ Back Office Review
→ Payout Calculated
→ Captain Receipt Generated
→ Owner and Captain Paid

These requirements are considered part of the MVP roadmap and should be incorporated into future booking workflow development.
